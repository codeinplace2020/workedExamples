As we all know, there are age restrictions in all kinds of matters -- playing video games, casting votes, and even to register in Code in Place :'). For the purpose of this problem, we are going to think about the age limit that is used most of the time -- 18 years, which is the age for voting in most countries.

Write a program that continuously takes a person's age as input and determines if a person can vote or not, that is, if he is older than 18 years old or not. If you want to finish the program, enter ctrl+c. 

    Enter your age: 12
    Sorry, you can't vote yet!
    
    Enter your age: 21
    You're old enough to vote!
